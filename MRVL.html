Bottom Line

Compelling AI Infrastructure Story — But Priced for Perfection

Marvell has executed a remarkable transformation into an AI-native data infrastructure semiconductor company. FY26 was a breakout year: record $8.2B revenue (+42%), non-GAAP EPS nearly doubling, and data center becoming 74% of business. The FY27–28 growth roadmap (~$11B → $15B) is backed by visible custom ASIC demand, deepening hyperscaler relationships, and first-mover technology advantages in 1.6T optical interconnect and 2nm DSP. The risk, however, is in the price. At ~$147, MRVL now trades above most analyst targets and at 57x trailing earnings. The bull case requires flawless execution against an aggressive multi-year guide, with no slowdown in hyperscaler capex. A risk score of 58/100 (Moderate) reflects genuine AI tailwinds balanced against elevated valuation, customer concentration, and a high-beta profile that punishes any miss severely.
