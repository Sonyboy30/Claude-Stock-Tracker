Amazon is simultaneously the most compelling long-term AI infrastructure story and the most complex risk-reward proposition of the three. Bulls view this as peak investment cycle before an ROI harvest; bears see it as unchecked spending. At a risk score of 52/100, Amazon scores highest of the three, warranting a conviction position only for investors comfortable with a 2-3 year payoff horizon.
